Daulatabad Fort



  • Daulatabad Fort, which is also known as Devagiri was built in the 14th century. Situated at a distance of 16 km northwest of Aurangabad, this fort is considered one of the seven wonders of Maharashtra.

  • Among the most gorgeous forts in Maharashtra, Daulatabad was captured by various rulers, including the Mughals, the Marathas, and the Peshwas.

  • In 1724 AD, it came under the Nizams of Hyderabad.

  • In order to reach the fort, you have to climb about 750 steps, which makes it a great hike.

  • Location: MH SH 22, Daulatabad, Maharashtra 431002 Entry Fees: N/A Famous For: Sightseeing, Trekking, Photography
